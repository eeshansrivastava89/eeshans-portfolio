Hi! I'm **Eeshan**. Thanks for visiting this site. 

I currently lead a data science team at Starbucks focused on AI/ML, personalization, and experimentation.

Been in the field for 12+ years across engineering, data science, and technical leadership. CS undergrad, MBA. I don't have any ex-FAANG, or Ivy League credentials. Just passionate and creative guy. 

I've always been a builder and early adopter since the days of receiving trial software in free CDs in Chip magazines (yeah, that far back!). 

With a little help from the super-autocomplete tools, I've been building projects that combine [data science](https://howiprompt.eeshans.com/), [AI](https://localai.eeshans.com/), and a bit of [gamification](https://absim.eeshans.com/) to keep things fun. 

I'm not a hype-follower, so a lot of my content is about testing new technologies and seeing what remains after the hype cycle passes. Which is why I'm particularly passionate about [Local AI](/writing) — models that run on your own devices, with full data ownership and fine-tuned control over model behavior.

My hope is that each project and post is useful, and teaches you something.

All my work is [open source](https://github.com/eeshansrivastava89/) and non-commercial. If you learn by building too, feel free to [follow along on my Substack](https://theasymptotic.substack.com).

In my free time, I enjoy *kickboxing*, *painting*, *reading*, and *basketball*. I am also a [Ravenclaw](https://www.harrypotter.com/house/ravenclaw).
