About

I'm Eeshan Srivastava. I lead a data science team at Starbucks — AI/ML, personalization, experimentation. 12 years across engineering, data science, and leadership. CS undergrad, MBA.

I test new tech to see what holds up after the hype fades. That's why I care about local AI — models that run on your own hardware, with your own data. The cost and quality math is shifting faster than people realize.

5 Under 35 from the Weatherhead School of Management. I've talked about AI applications at UCLA and been a guest on two podcasts about data science careers. All my work is open source and non-commercial. Outside of work: kickboxing, painting, reading, basketball. Ravenclaw.
