I'm Eeshan Srivastava.

I lead a data science team at Starbucks, focused on AI/ML, personalization, and experimentation. I've been in the field for 12+ years across engineering, data science, and technical leadership. CS undergrad, MBA. I don't have ex-FAANG or Ivy League credentials — I got here by building things and paying attention to what works.

I test new tech to see what holds up after the hype fades. That's why I care about local AI — models running on your own hardware, with your own data, where you control what happens to it. The cost and quality math is shifting faster than most people realize.

All my work is open source and non-commercial. If you learn by building too, follow along on Asymptotic.

Outside of work: kickboxing, painting, reading, basketball. Ravenclaw.
